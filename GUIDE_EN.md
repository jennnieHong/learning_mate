## 3. Smart Review (Aggregated Wrong Answers)

One of LearningMate's most powerful features is the **Review Mode**.

1. On the home screen, use **checkboxes** to select the files you want to review (or select all).
2. If there are incorrect answers in the selected files, a **Review Dashboard** will appear at the top.
3. Click **'Start Review Mode'** to practice only the missed questions across all selected files.
